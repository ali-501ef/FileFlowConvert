Why Split PDFs?

PDF splitting is crucial for document organization and sharing efficiency. Large documents often contain multiple sections that serve different purposes or audiences. By splitting PDFs, you can share relevant sections without overwhelming recipients, reduce file sizes for faster transmission, and organize content for better accessibility and navigation.
