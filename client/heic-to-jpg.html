Why Convert HEIC to JPG?

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is Apple's modern image format that saves space but isn't universally supported. Converting to JPG ensures your photos work everywhere - on Windows PCs, Android devices, older browsers, and web platforms. This conversion is essential for sharing photos with non-Apple users and uploading to websites that don't support HEIC format.
